Publications

My peer-reviewed research and undergraduate research contributions.

Geophysical Investigation of Groundwater Potential Using Advance Magneto Telluric (ADMT-200S-X) Around Pindiga and Environs, Upper Benue Trough, Northeastern Nigeria.

IRE Journals • Peer Reviewed

Co-authored peer-reviewed research investigating groundwater potential in Pindiga, Northeastern Nigeria using Advanced Magnetotelluric (ADMT-200S-X) geophysical surveys. The study identified promising aquifer zones through subsurface resistivity analysis, providing scientific evidence for sustainable groundwater development and improved borehole siting to address water scarcity in the region.
